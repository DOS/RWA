Where Qwen actually earns its keep

1

It reads the prospectus

PDFs go in, 15+ structured fields come out — name, symbol, face value, coupon, maturity, restrictions. The issuer reviews and edits; they never fill a form from scratch.

2

It checks the law

Same model, different prompt: is the issuer registered in Vietnam? Is KYC enforced? Are the numbers sane? The compliance report either greenlights the deploy button or explains why it's blocking it.

3

It advises investors

A chat panel in the investor portal, fluent in Vietnamese, English, and Korean. Answers "which bond fits my profile?" using on-chain data — not marketing copy.

4

It writes the regulator report

Compliance officers get a document they can actually hand to SSC — issuer status, claim registry, transfer audit trail, all stitched together. Exports to PDF.

Deploys to the bank's own servers. We run Qwen through vLLM on Shinhan's GPUs if they want it. Prospectuses and investor data never leave the network perimeter.
